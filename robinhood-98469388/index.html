This Trademark is a registered trademark (Registration #98785432) owned by Robinhood Markets, Inc., a Menlo Park based entity located in CA. The trademark was filed on 26 Mar 2024 with serial number (#98469388) and registered on 10 Jul 2026. The trademark is filed in the category of Insurance & Financial Services. The company began using the mark in commerce on 10th July 2026. This mark identifies Robinhood’s proprietary infrastructure for minting and deploying EVM-compatible tokens on Robinhood Chain. As of 10 Jul 2026, the trademark remains Live/Registered, with a recent status, REGISTERED. The mark is represented by attorney Calla E. Yee of law firm Calla E.
